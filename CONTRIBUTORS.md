# ChatCommons contributors

ChatCommons recognizes code, protocol, security, documentation, design,
translation, testing, and product feedback as real contributions.

## Product and design feedback

Early reviewers helped clarify the project mission, improve hierarchy and
legibility, design scalable community and room navigation, and strengthen the
feedback workflow. Their preferred public credits will be added here after they
opt in.

## Adding a credit

A contributor entry records only what the person wants to publish:

```text
- Preferred name or handle — contribution area (optional profile link)
```

Do not copy an account name from a private conversation or screenshot into this
file without consent. Anonymous contributors may be credited as “Anonymous” or
may decline a public entry entirely. Credit does not require code or a pull
request, and inclusion does not transfer copyright beyond the repository's
normal contribution terms.
